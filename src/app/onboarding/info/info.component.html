Cross-chain accounts
Klaster cross-chain accounts enable any MutliSig Wallet, deployed on a single blockchain - to send / receive assets & execute transactions on multiple blockchains. Learn more
Klaster cross-chain accounts are fully trustless. Only the owners of the wallet can access the funds.
