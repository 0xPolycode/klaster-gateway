Cross-chain subaccounts
A Klaster cross-chain account is a blockchain address which is controlled by your wallet or smart contract.

It enables smart contracts, such as Safe multisigs, DAOs or dApps to control assets on remote blockchains.

Click continue to calculate your cross-chain account address. The address is derived from your wallet or contract address.

The process takes under a minute and requires no transactions or signing.
Klaster subaccounts are fully trustless. Only the owner wallet or contract can access the subaccount.
